🎉 Summary Notes: What We Learned About Forms!

🌟 Forms are the way we talk to websites!
They help users send information like names, bookings, and orders to the website.

🧠 Key things to remember:
✅ Use the <form> tag to start your form.
✅ Always use <label> for each input — it tells users what to type.
✅ <input> is for typing information (text, email, password, etc.).
✅ <select> lets users choose from a list (like selecting a food item).
✅ <textarea> is for longer text — like comments or special instructions.
✅ Don’t forget a <button> to submit your form! 🚀
